About

The Oklahoma Sooners have made waves in the college football scene since they joined in 1895. Their trophy room is giant, holding 7 national championships, 50 conference championships, 167 All American teams, and 7 Heisman Trophy winners. They have recently transferred from the BIG 12 Conference to the SEC, where they have high hopes. The current head coach of the Oklahoma Sooners is Brent Venables, replacing former head coach Lincoln Riley when he transfered to USC

Top Athletes

Baker Mayfield

Baker Mayfield is a legendary quarterback in the NFL. Mayfield set the NFL rookie quarterback record for passing touchdowns during his first season, along with ending a 19-game winless streak for the Browns in his debut. He continued to impress Cleveland Browns fans until ultimately being traded to the Tampa Bay Buccaneers where he is today.

Caleb Williams

Caleb Williams is an up-and-coming star on the NFL scene. While playing for Oklahoma and USC he won AP Player of the Year, the Maxwell Award, and the 2022 Heisman Trophy. He was drafted to the Chicago Bears in the 2024 NFL draft.

Ceedee Lamb

Ceedee Lamb is one of the most talented Wide Receivers on the NFL scene. He has won First-team All-Pro, Second-team All-Pro, 3x Pro Bowl, NFL receptions leader, Consensus All-American, First-team All-Big 12, and Second-team All-Big 12. He is currently on the Dallas Cowboys and is performing beautifully.
